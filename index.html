About Me

How to Initiate Services

Next Steps to Begin

1 - Initial Discovery Session:

Schedule a free initial consultation with us, available by phone or virtually. This introductory session is an opportunity for you to discuss your concerns and ask questions, allowing us to understand your needs and determine if our services are the right fit.


2 - In-Depth Assessment:

We will arrange a thorough evaluation, which includes reviewing medical history, observing clinical behavior, conducting assessment measures, and interviewing caregivers and teachers if needed. This evaluation is conducted with cultural and linguistic sensitivity, using both informal and formal assessments. If your child has been evaluated in the last 6 months, a re-evaluation will be scheduled instead. Following the evaluation, we will discuss the results and provide personalized recommendations.


3 - Start Your Therapy Journey:

After reviewing the most effective therapy plan for your child, we’ll go over available session times that fit your schedule. Our therapy sessions are designed to be engaging, interactive, and tailored to meet each child’s unique interests and needs, ensuring a positive and productive experience.
